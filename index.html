Hello! My name is Maria del Carmen Santiago, and I am a Madrid-based junior web developer. In the last few months I have been building up my skills as a full-stack developer by completing a bootcamp and my own projects.

I would say that my front-end skills are stronger right now, but I have worked with back-end development as well and am not afraid to learn more about either. Go to 'Projects' to have a peak into some of my work. I also invite you to visit my github page at github.com/mc-santiago/ to see more.

What has been my web-dev educational journey like this year?

Well, I started learning about HTML and CSS. Easy enough... then came javascript. Not as easy, but so much more fun! After javascript came the part about learning about the oh so famous js frameworks. I started with React and VUE. I have been doing most of my projects with ReactJS, but after taking a workshop and building my first VUE project I got to say I am glad I started with the hardest of the two to understand!
